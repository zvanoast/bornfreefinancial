J. C. Penney Company, Inc., through its subsidiary, J. C. Penney Corporation, Inc., operates department stores. It sells family apparel and footwear, accessories, fine and fashion jewelry, beauty products, and home furnishings. The company also provides various services, such as styling salon, optical, portrait photography, and custom decorating. As of January 20, 2014, it operated 1,100 stores. The company also sells its products through its Internet Website, jcp.com. J. C. Penney Company, Inc. was founded in 1902 and is based in Plano, Texas.
